about us

Annex builds things.
Not the kind of things that you can touch.
We build things that touch you.
Your mind,
your heart,
your spirit.

When we build, we wield the stuff of dreams.

Our tools are code
and light
and imagination.
We learn. We listen. We research. We advance our art and our technology to connect people.

The things we build inspire and excite. They convert customers to fans, visitors to guests, brands to icons.

Our charge is to bring all of this together:
technology, design, experience, knowledge.
And when we add you, your story, and your goals to the mix, something magical happens:

Annex creates.

Annex is an integrated creative consultancy in Charleston, S.C. As core services, we provide design and strategy services with an emphasis on web design and development, brand identity development and strategy, and social media engagement. And, we're ready to work with you.
